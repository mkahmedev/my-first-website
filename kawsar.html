About Sylhet

Sylhet, known as the "City of Saints" and for its natural beauty, offers diverse attractions for tourists. It's famous for its lush tea gardens, rolling hills, and historical sites like the mausoleum of Hazrat Shah Jalal. Visitors can explore the scenic Jaflong area with its river and mountains, or enjoy the serene Ratargul Swamp Forest.
